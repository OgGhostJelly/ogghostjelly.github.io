Hot Cocoa

go back

Hot cocoa but extremely over-engineered. I kind-of just.. kept adding more ingredients.

ingredients

  • milk - 270g
  • vanilla extract - 1/8 tsp
  • cocoa powder - 2 tsp
  • creamer - 1 tsp
  • sugar - 1 1/4 tsp
  • corn starch - 1/4 tsp
  • salt - 1 pinch

thats every ingredient. thats a lot of ingredients..

directions

  1. mix it together
  2. microwave for a few seconds
  3. repeat mix and microwave until its completely dissolved

while you drink think about even more ingredients you can potentially add to it
