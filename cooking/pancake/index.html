English pancakes, they're the thin crêpe-like ones not the thick American ones.

ingredients

  • flour - 100g
  • eggs - 2
  • milk - 300g
  • oil - 1 tbsp
  • salt - 1 pinch

very minimal amount of ingredients, unlike my over-engineered hot cocoa

directions

  1. mix the ingredients together
  2. if the flour wont fully disolve, just let it sit for 10 minutes
