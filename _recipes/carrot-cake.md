---
title: Carrot Cake
description: The frosting is not optional.
image_src: /assets/images/recipe/carrot-cake.jpg


ingredients:
  - header: Batter

  - item: Flour
    amount: 1 3/4 cups
  - item: Baking Powder
    amount: 1 1/2tsp
  - item: Baking Soda
    amount: 1tsp
  - item: Cinnamon
    amount: 1/2tsp
  - item: Eggs
    amount: 4
  - item: Vegetable Oil
    amount: 1 cup
  - item: Sugar
    amount: 3/4 cup
  - item: Carrot (shredded)
    amount: 2 cups
  - item: Vanilla
    amount: 1tsp


  - header: Frosting

  - item: Sugar
    amount: 1/2 cup
  - item: Butter
    amount: 1/2 cup
  - item: Cream Cheese
    amount: 1 cup
  - item: Sour Cream
    amount: 1/2 cup
  - item: Vanilla
    amount: 1tsp


directions:
  - header: Batter

  - Mix eggs and vegetable oil in a bowl.
  - Slowly mix in the flour, baking soda, baking powder and cinnamon.
  - Mix the rest of the ingredients into the bowl.
  - Place a piece of cut out parchment paper at the bottom of a pan.
  - Butter or oil the pan, then pour in the batter.
  - Preheat an oven at 175 °C
  - Cook for 45min at 175 °C
  - Take the cake out of the oven and wait for it to cool.


  - header: Frosting

  - Mix the ingredients in a bowl.
---
