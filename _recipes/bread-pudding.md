---
title: Bread Pudding
description: Great for getting rid of stale bread!
image_src: /assets/images/recipe/bread-pudding.jpg


ingredients:
  - item: Bread
    amount: 275g
  - item: Butter
    amount: 30g
  - item: Milk
    amount: 400g
  - item: Sugar
    amount: 100g
  - item: Raisins
    amount: 80g
  - item: Eggs
    amount: 3
  - item: Cinnamon
    amount: 0.5tsp
  - item: Vanilla (extract)
    amount: 0.5tsp


directions:
  - Pour the ingredients into a bowl.
  - Mix thoroughly.
  - Pour the batter into a pan.
  - Cook for 45min at 175 °C.
  - Serve and eat with cream!
---
