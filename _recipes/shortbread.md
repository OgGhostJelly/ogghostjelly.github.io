---
title: Shortbread
description: Delicious! Usually Served with tea.
image_src: /assets/images/recipe/shortbread.jpg


ingredients:
  - item: Butter
    amount: 100g
  - item: Sugar
    amount: 40g
  - item: Flour
    amount: 150g
  - item: Corn Starch
    amount: 1tsp
  - item: Baking Powder
    amount: 0.25tsp


directions:
  - Pour ingredients into a bowl
  - Mix until smooth
  - Put in a pan
  - Cook for 40mins at 160 °C
  - Wait to cool
---
