---
title: Hot Chocolate
description: Hot chocolate with way too many ingredients.
image_src: /assets/images/recipe/hot-chocolate.jpg


ingredients:
  - item: Milk
    amount: 270g
  - item: Vanilla (extract)
    amount: 0.125tsp
  - item: Cocoa
    amount: 2tsp
  - item: Creamer
    amount: 1tsp
  - item: Sugar
    amount: 1.25tsp
  - item: Corn Starch
    amount: 0.25tsp
  - item: Salt
    amount: 1 pinch


directions:
  - Mix ingredients thoroughly.
  - Microwave the hot chocolate for 30 seconds.
  - Repeat 1 and 2 until the ingredients have dissolved.
  - Enjoy!
---
