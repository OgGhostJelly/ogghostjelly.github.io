---
title: Pancakes
description: English pancakes/Crepes. Yes, there is a difference between American and English pancakes.
preview_src: /assets/images/recipe/previews/pancakes.jpg


ingredients:
  - item: Flour
    amount: 100g
  - item: Eggs
    amount: 2
  - item: Milk
    amount: 300g
  - item: Oil
    amount: 1tbsp
  - item: Salt
    amount: 1 pinch


directions:
  - Pour ingredients into a bowl
  - Mix until smooth
  - Cook for 1min 30sec on each side<br>If the pancakes break easily while flipping them, add more egg
  - Eat!
---
